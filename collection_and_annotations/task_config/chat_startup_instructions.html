Welcome to the Dialogue Behavior Analysis assignment!


In this session, you will participate in two dialogues and answer questions about each.

Please read the instructions carefully before each task in order to complete them correctly. Due to the interactive nature of this task, your progress cannot be intermediately saved, so please be sure you have time to complete this in one sitting (approximately 10 minutes).



Please note that these dialogues will be used for research and released to the public. While we encourage you to speak in a natural manner as if talking to a friend or acquaintance, feel free to fabricate any personal details you are not comfortable sharing.

In particular, please be careful not to reveal any personally identifiable information (address, phone number, etc.). If you do have a concern with anything you have shared in one of the dialogues, contact jdfinch@emory.edu and we will work with you to resolve the issue.
